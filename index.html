II. About / The idea • UniTracker / Term 01 002 / 008
About the app · Nº 02

Made by students who got tired of the chaos.

Most students keep a degree spread across a notes app, a calendar, a group chat, and memory. UniTracker brings it together: courses, tasks, attendance, and notes in one place. Your data stays private — we can’t see it, other students can’t, and we never sell it.

Method · Nº 05

From a messy syllabus to a term you can see.

+

Four habits, repeated each term. UniTracker makes each one take seconds instead of an afternoon.

01

Capture →

Add your courses, credits, and key dates once. Setup takes a few minutes, then it’s done.

02

Organize →

Every task lands on your calendar automatically, sorted by what’s due first.

03

Track →

Mark attendance and check off work as you go. Where you stand is always one tap away.

04

Improve
